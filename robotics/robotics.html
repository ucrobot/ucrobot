Robot

A robot is a machine-especially one programmable by a computer-capable fo carrying out a complex series of actions automatically. [1]

Robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science and engineering. Robotics involves design, construction, operation, and use of robots. [2]
